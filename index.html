WOMEN IN STEM

...and gender minorities in STEM!

why should you join?

  • meet a community of gender minorities in STEM
  • access to volunteering opportunities (such as culminating interest in stem in young girls)
  • WiSTEM Mentorship Program: guidance with a female university student in the same field as you
  • WiSTEM Alumni Network: contact info for students in schools like Harvard, Princeton, Yale, and more!
  • planning fundraisers like bake sales!
  • leadership positions for all grades!
  • fun discussions!
  • more info about WiSTEM can be found athttps://womeninstem.org
